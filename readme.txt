DonkeyKong Adventures:

Instructions:
Move DonkeyKong by tilting the phone and try to collect all bananas. Avoid barrels, these will cost you a life and try to jump over water by double taping the screen. If you fall into it, DonkeyKong dies and your game is over.
Further you can collect hearts to fill up your health bar and get lives.

Features:
- Sensor control and movement
- Player animations
- Rewards
- HUD
- DonkeyKong side moves and jumping
- Parallaxing Background
- Different Screens
